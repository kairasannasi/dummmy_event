    About Us

    Dr. E.K. Suresh Group of Educational Institutions was established in 2022 in the rural area of Cuddalore District. As co-educational self-financed institutions, we are located in Viruddhachalam with a mission to offer various value-based programs that align with global requirements. Our institutions are dedicated to providing quality education through innovative teaching methodologies and a holistic approach.

    Our flagship institutions include:

    • Sri Saraswathi Vidhyalaya Matriculation Higher Secondary School
    • Sri Saraswathi Vidhyalaya International School (CBSE)
    • CSM Arts & Science College
    • Indhumathi Suresh College of Education

    We believe that our students are our most valuable assets. We strive to create an environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. Our campuses are equipped with modern facilities and are set in beautiful, natural surroundings, providing the perfect atmosphere for learning and development.
